About Net SNMP

Simple Network Management Protocol (SNMP) is a widely used protocol for monitoring the health and welfare of network equipment (e.g. routers), computer equipment and even devices like UPS.

GroundWork Monitor SNMP Trap processing uses Net SNMP libraries.
